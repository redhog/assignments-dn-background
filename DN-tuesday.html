Role Success

What matters most to succeed in this role, and how it should work practically to deliver value.

Well-Defined Goals

  • Clear short- and long-term goals are essential in a broad role
  • Translate ambiguity into concrete, measurable outcomes

Iterative Refinement

  • Revisit and refine goals continuously
  • Apply agile thinking to role impact and scope

Hard Prioritization

  • Optimize for impact across product teams
  • Minimize duplicate effort and functionality
  • Shared model services over per-team models
  • Identify shared libraries opportunities

Avoid Overengineering

  • Match solutions to current team size
  • Favor pragmatic, evolvable architectures

Start with Learning

  • Understand current datasets, systems, and processes
  • Identify low-hanging fruit
  • Course-correct based on outcomes
